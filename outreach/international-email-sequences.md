# PixelorCode — International Email Sequences (US/AU Health Practices)
_v1, 2026-06-11. Pairs with the verifier verdicts in `outputs/us_au_health_leads/`._
_Send from hello@pixelorcode.com (Zoho). 10-20/day max during manual warmup. Plain text, no images, no links in email #1 except signature._

## Rules (proven in India, adapted for US/AU)
- "Hi [Practice Name] team" — org greeting, no guessed first names (use the doctor's name ONLY if it's on the practice's own site)
- ONE verifiable broken thing per email. They must be able to confirm it in 10 seconds.
- Their reputation as contrast ("your patients clearly love you — your website disagrees")
- US spelling/tone for US, slightly warmer for AU. Short. No "Dear Sir". No "I hope this email finds you well".
- Signature: Rishav · PixelorCode · pixelorcode.com (plain text)
- Subject lines: lowercase, specific, non-salesy.

## Loom script (90 seconds, top-15 prospects)
1. (0-10s) "Hi {Practice} team, Rishav from PixelorCode — recorded this for you specifically, 90 seconds."
2. (10-40s) Screen: Google search for them → click their site → THE BROKEN THING happening live. Narrate calmly, no gloating.
3. (40-75s) Screen: the concept mockup. "This is what we'd replace it with — booking button, your reviews, your services."
4. (75-90s) "If you want this, just reply — we'll have it live in three weeks. Either way, the concept is yours."
